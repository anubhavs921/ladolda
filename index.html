Flour

We import 100% organic flour from
Italy, sourcing it from the best Italian
mills. Our flour is certified organic
and non-GMO.

Extrusion Process

Only bronze dies are used for the
extrusion process, giving the pasta
the ideal “rough” texture that enables
sauces to cling to the pasta.

Drying

Drying takes place at low temperatures for over 18 hours, to preserve the nutritional and sensory qualities of the product.

Packaging

We package our pasta, by hand, in order to ensure a delicate packaging process.

Shipping

We carefully package our pasta to protect it, from our kitchen to yours.
